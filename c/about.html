About Keru Beats

Welcome to Keru Beats, your go-to DJ service for unforgettable music experiences!

Based in Krugersdorp, Gauteng, we bring the beats that move and the music that speaks. Whether you’re hosting a private party or club night, we deliver high-energy performances tailored to your vibe.

Our Standard Package features a professional DJ set with a JBL PartyBox sound system, ensuring quality sound at an affordable rate. For those looking to elevate their event, our Premium Package includes lighting, smoke effects, and an additional subwoofer for a full sensory experience.
